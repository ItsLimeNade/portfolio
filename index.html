About Me

Hi, I'm LimeNade! I'm a young French developer with a passion for tinkering with electronics and writing code. My journey began at age 8 when I created my first Scratch account. It got me hooked on coding as a kid, and I've never stopped since!

I love learning new things and constantly try to expand my knowledge across different fields of engineering. I experiment with soldering, PCB design, web apps, web games, and even mobile development!

I truly believe that open-source software is the way forward.
